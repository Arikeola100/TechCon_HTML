History

TechCon began in 2010 with a mission to bring together the best and brightest in technology. Over the years, it has grown from a small gathering of enthusiasts to a major international event, attracting thousands of attendees from all over the world.

In its early years, TechCon focused on emerging technologies and providing a platform for up-and-coming tech startups. The inaugural conference was held in a modest venue with just a few hundred attendees, but the response was overwhelmingly positive. This encouraged the organizers to expand the scope and scale of the event.

As the conference evolved, it began to attract high-profile speakers and established tech companies. By 2015, TechCon had become a significant event on the tech industry calendar, known for its cutting-edge content and networking opportunities. The conference moved to larger venues to accommodate the growing number of participants, exhibitors, and speakers.

Today, TechCon is held annually in San Francisco, a hub of technological innovation. It features an extensive program that includes keynote speeches, panel discussions, workshops, and an exhibition hall showcasing the latest advancements in technology. The event continues to uphold its founding mission while adapting to the rapid changes in the tech landscape

Mission

TechCon's mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and education within the technology sector. The conference provides a platform for tech professionals, enthusiasts, and companies to share their knowledge, showcase their innovations, and build meaningful connections.

At its core, TechCon aims to:

  • Inspire Innovation: By presenting the latest advancements and breakthroughs in technology, TechCon seeks to inspire attendees to push the boundaries of what's possible.
  • Promote Collaboration: The conference encourages networking and partnerships among attendees, creating opportunities for collaboration that can lead to significant advancements and new ventures.
  • Facilitate Education: Through its diverse program of workshops, tutorials, and panel discussions, TechCon offers valuable learning experiences for professionals at all levels. Attendees gain practical skills and knowledge that they can apply in their work and projects.
  • Support the Tech Community: TechCon is committed to supporting the tech community by providing a platform for startups and innovators to gain visibility and connect with potential investors and partners.
